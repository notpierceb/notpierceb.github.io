Nice to meet you!

Welcome to my website!!!! Whether you're from Europe, America, or wherever, you can always come to my small chunk of the internet.

WELCOME to my small tribute to the vibrant internet of the past. :)

I love the internet, but I dislike how over time, its focus shifted from a place of vibrant personal expression to a hell-scape infested with corporate art and advertisements. I made this website to be a tribute to what the internet once was.

Anyways, more about me, I'm Pierce! On a good day, you might find me eating junk food, trying to fish at my local aquarium, and casting spells. I am a second year computer science student, and I also love playing my guitar.

On my endlessly under-construction website, you might find anything related to me and my hobbies, such as music, anime, side projects, and random thoughts about things. Im lazy so I hope I get around to doing this.
